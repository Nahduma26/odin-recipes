Thai Chicken Wraps

Description

Thai Chicken Lettuce Wraps are a delectable and refreshing culinary experience that showcases the vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine in a light and health-conscious manner. This dish reimagines the traditional wrap by replacing the usual carb-heavy tortilla or bread with crisp and fresh lettuce leaves, creating a wholesome and satisfying alternative.

At the core of these wraps is tender and seasoned chicken, often marinated in a delightful blend of Thai-inspired ingredients such as soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and a touch of chili for a hint of heat. The result is succulent, flavorful chicken that becomes the star of the dish.

The wraps gain their Thai identity from the accompanying elements that contribute to the overall taste profile. A zesty peanut sauce, made with ingredients like peanut butter, lime juice, and soy sauce, adds a rich and nutty flavor, creating a harmonious blend with the savory chicken. Fresh herbs such as cilantro and mint bring a burst of aromatic freshness, while finely chopped vegetables like carrots and bell peppers provide a satisfying crunch.

The lettuce leaves, acting as the vessel for this culinary creation, offer a refreshing and crisp backdrop to the bold and savory filling. The wraps are assembled by spooning the flavorful chicken mixture onto the lettuce leaves, drizzling with the luscious peanut sauce, and garnishing with a generous sprinkle of herbs and vegetables.

Thai Chicken Lettuce Wraps not only deliver a burst of Thai-inspired flavors but also cater to those seeking a lighter, carb-conscious option. The combination of savory, nutty, and fresh elements creates a well-rounded and satisfying dish, perfect for a light lunch, appetizer, or even a health-conscious dinner. With its vibrant colors, diverse textures, and a symphony of Thai tastes, these lettuce wraps are a delicious embodiment of the fusion between health-conscious choices and the bold flavors of Thai cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (or tofu)
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Carrots
  • Cabbage
  • Green Onions
  • Sweet Chili Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Peanut Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Cilantro Leaves
  • Lettuce Leaves

Steps

  1. Cook Chicken: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium. Add chicken, garlic, onions, salt and pepper and cook, stirring occasionally, until chicken is cooked through.
  2. Add Veggies: Add the carrots, cabbage, and green onions, cooking an additional 4-5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  3. Make Sauce: In a small bowl combine the sweet chili sauce, ginger, chunky peanut butter, and soy sauce, and crushed red pepper. Stir until smooth.
  4. Combine: Add to pan along with chopped cilantro. Stir until blended in and heated through.
  5. Serve: Spoon mixture into lettuce leaves and enjoy these Thai ground chicken lettuce wraps!
